Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?
You can browse our shop, add items to your cart, and complete checkout to place an order.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and times vary depending on location.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept mobile money, bank transfer, and card payments via our secure payment gateway.
How do I track my order?
After checkout, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp or email to track your order status.
Can I return or exchange items?
Yes, we have a 7-day return/exchange policy for eligible items. Contact us via WhatsApp to initiate a return.
